# Investor-Safe Summary (Baseline)

## Claims safe to use now (high confidence)
1. **IDEA population has grown materially over the last decade** (NCES: 6.4M to 7.5M from 2012-13 to 2022-23).
2. **FERPA/HIPAA boundary supports treating school-held student records as FERPA-governed education records** (government guidance).
3. **AI competition in education includes low-cost/freemium offerings**, reinforcing the need for clear differentiation beyond generic AI tooling.

## Claims to hold back until corroborated
- National/state dispute trend percentages and concentration shares.
- Any legal phrasing that sounds absolute across all districts/states.

## Suggested safe narrative
Advocedu targets a large and growing special-education population with persistent coordination burden, using a FERPA-aligned parent workflow. The immediate validation priority is proving retention and outcomes in an email-first service model, while hardening state-level dispute and legal-operability evidence.
